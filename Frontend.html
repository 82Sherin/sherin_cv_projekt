Full Stack Developer Program — with a Unique Focus on Security I completed a 400-credit Higher Vocational Education program in Full Stack Development at Företagsuniversitetet, designed to provide both the depth and breadth required to manage and execute all parts of an IT project.
The program emphasized building secure, scalable, and testable IT solutions—skills that are highly relevant for roles such as programmer, IT consultant, or software developer.

The training began with a solid foundation in software development and advanced step by step, enabling students to independently design and deliver complete IT solutions.

We worked extensively with modern technologies and frameworks, including:

HTML, CSS, and responsive web design

React and Angular for front-end development

Node.js, Java, and Spring for back-end applications

Agile methodologies and version control systems

SQL and NoSQL databases

And a strong focus on information security and system reliability

What truly made the program unique was its security perspective—an area often overlooked in similar programs—and its flexible study format (75% pace over 2.5 years), allowing students to gain professional experience alongside their studies.

Around 50–60% of the coursework involved hands-on programming, striking the right balance between theory and practical application.

Collaboration with Leading Companies

The program also included LIA (internship) placements at major organizations, providing valuable real-world experience.
Through Företagsuniversitetet’s strong partnerships, students gained access to companies such as Truecaller, Atea, the Swedish Armed Forces, Region Stockholm, and the Swedish National Agency for Education (Skolverket).

This professional collaboration offered the opportunity to specialize in areas of personal interest and build networks in both consulting firms and global organizations.

Preparing for the Future

The Full Stack Developer program is designed for those with a genuine passion for IT and a drive to build future-ready skills. Becoming a full stack developer means mastering both front-end and back-end development—a challenge that makes you especially valuable in today’s job market.

Full stack developers are often described as “unicorns”—rare, versatile, and highly sought after. This program was created to train exactly that kind of professional: someone capable of seeing the bigger picture, bridging multiple technologies, and developing secure, high-quality digital solutions.
